Frequently Asked Questions

What is Achievement Management System?

It is a centralized platform designed to help students and teachers track academic achievements efficiently.

Who can use this platform?

Both students and teachers can use this system for recording and monitoring academic accomplishments.

Can certificates be uploaded?

Yes, users can upload digital certificates which remain accessible anytime.

Is my data secure?

The system uses authentication and structured data storage practices to keep information secure.
